Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Our standard delivery time is 30-45 minutes for most areas. During peak hours, it may take up to 60 minutes.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! You can select your preferred ingredients and spice levels when building your meal on our website.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering for events and parties. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance for catering orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and all major digital payment platforms including PayPal and Apple Pay.

Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, there's a minimum order of ₦25,000 for delivery and ₦15,000 for pickup orders.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. You can customize any dish to be vegetarian-friendly.
